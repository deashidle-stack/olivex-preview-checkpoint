## What This Prototype Covers

- OliveX product card with 2 planned size slots
- Dedicated OliveX product page with analysebevis status and source-honest size placeholders
- Browser-native review guide for client/contact walkthrough
- Browser-native claim review for legal/brand decisions before production copy
- Browser-native production intake for products, COA, media, claims, Shopify, Vipps and Posten/Bring data
- Browser-native launch checklist for Shopify, checkout, shipping, SEO, support, QA and training
- Product-page size comparison for two planned sizes without invented ml/prices
- Product 2 slot with 2 placeholder size variants pending client product data
- Cart drawer and checkout UI scaffold
- Purchase-plan selector for one-time purchase versus fast delivery as UX direction
- Mobile sticky product-page purchase bar with disabled add-to-cart until real prices exist
- Preview-safe cookie consent banner with reject/accept/custom choices
- Vipps and card checkout choices as frontend placeholders
- Posten/Bring shipping story
- Product-page-ready analysebevis/batch-information pattern
- SEO metadata and product structured data
- Preview-safe `noindex,nofollow`
- Guarded support-assistant preview
- Mobile-first responsive layout
- Norwegian OliveX source-copy direction with production/legal review notes

## Production Recommendation

Use Shopify for the live build unless the client has a strong WordPress/content requirement.

- Shopify Payments for card payments in Norway
- Official Vipps/MobilePay Shopify plugin
- Posten Bring Checkout Shopify app
- Shopify SSL, products, variants, inventory, orders, refunds and admin training

This prototype should be translated into Shopify sections after product data, brand assets and legal copy review are complete.
