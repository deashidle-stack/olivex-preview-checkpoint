# Legal And Compliance Pages

The Shopify build should include these pages before launch.

Browser-native review page:

- `legal-policy-readiness.html`

Detailed current checklist:

- `docs/LEGAL_POLICY_READINESS_2026-06-07.md`

## Required Pages

- Kjøpsvilkår
- Personvernerklæring
- Cookiepolicy
- Frakt og levering
- Retur og angrerett
- Kontakt
- Om selskapet
- Kvalitet og dokumentasjon
- Fast levering / subscription terms if recurring delivery is in v1
- Support/agent scope if a live AI support flow is enabled

## Footer Requirements

Footer should include:

- Legal company name
- Organization number
- Postal address
- Contact email
- Links to legal pages
- Links to shipping/returns
- Links to privacy/cookies and support/contact

## Product Page Compliance

Before purchase, customers should see:

- legal product name/category
- net quantity
- ingredients
- allergens
- recommended daily dose
- warnings
- storage
- batch/certificate information where relevant
- seller/importer details available in footer/legal pages
- link to terms/returns before checkout where appropriate

## Cookie/Analytics Setup

If using analytics or marketing pixels:

- show accept/reject choices
- do not fire non-essential tags before consent
- connect Google Consent Mode if Google tags are used
- document which cookies are used and why

## No-Go Before Launch

- No visible legal seller/importer details.
- Tracking or marketing pixels fire before consent.
- Subscription/fast delivery is promised without terms and payment testing.
- Delivery time or shipping price is invented before Posten/Bring setup.
- Product labeling fields are missing or based on assumptions.
- Health/medical claims are unresolved.

## Important

All legal text should be reviewed by the client or a legal advisor. The theme and docs provide structure, not legal approval.
