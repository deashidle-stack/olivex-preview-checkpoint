# Product Data Checklist

## Required Before Final Copy

For each SKU:

- Legal product name
- Category: food, supplement, fortified food, novel food or other
- Net quantity
- Serving size
- Recommended daily dose
- Number of servings
- Ingredients in descending order
- Allergens
- Active substances per daily dose
- Nutrition declaration where required
- Storage
- Best-before handling
- Batch/lot structure
- Manufacturer/importer name and postal address
- Warnings
- Certificate or COA files

## Olive Oil Specific

- Olive oil category
- Country, region and estate
- Cultivar
- Harvest date/year
- Milling and extraction method
- Bottle date
- Polyphenol result in mg/kg
- Hydroxytyrosol and derivatives per 20 g
- Lab name
- Test method
- Test date
- Certificate URL
- Storage away from heat and light

## Claim Guardrails

Safe direction:

- Batch-tested composition
- Origin and production method
- Measured active substances
- Authorized substance-level health claims when conditions are met
- Usage, dosage and transparency

Avoid:

- Disease prevention or treatment claims
- LDL reduction, blood pressure, diabetes, cancer, Alzheimer, detox or anti-inflammatory language unless specifically authorized
- Doctor-style endorsement imagery
- Before/after framing
- Generic antioxidant marketing unless legally reviewed

Olive oil polyphenol claim must only be used when the measured composition qualifies and the 20 g daily intake context is present.
